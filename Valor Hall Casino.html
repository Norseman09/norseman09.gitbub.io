Nine Sided Craps

Game Rules

Craps with two mythical nine-sided dice. The rules are simple:

  • The Gods will not favor a 9 or 13.
  • They favor two of the same number.
  • All other numbers grant you a fortunate reroll.

May the odds be ever in your favor.
